Rising Entrepreneur

 24th April, 2021    4:00 pm

A competition for a cause. Unlike other competitions where the participation is for winning and gaining experience this competition will promote, encourage and foster the idea of social entrepreneurship which is defined as an approach by individuals, groups, start-up companies or entrepreneurs, in which they develop, fund and implement solutions to social, cultural, or environmental issues. In this competition the same will be expected from the participants to come up with an idea that resolves an issue pertaining to a product and motivates you to fight for a cause.

Rising Entrepreneur

Rising Entrepreneur: BloomBox plans to organize an event to promote the awareness of Social Entrepreneurship in students in Collaboration with Enactus, The Somaiya Social Cell. Enactus is a student body created to undertake Social Activities in the campus of K. J. Somaiya Institute of Management Studies and Research. It serves the underprivileged by creating opportunities for sustainable development & developing entrepreneurial spirit. This event will consist of 2 rounds, for round 1 the participants will be given a product from one of the projects of Enactus and they have to prepare a thorough document by explaining the start to end life cycle of the product. Round 2 will consist of selected top teams who will be asked questions on the basis of the submitted document and also will be given a problem statement based on Social Entrepreneurship to solve. The main idea behind this is to focus more on the social entrepreneurship part, its scope and importance

Event Flow:

  1. A Speaker Session explaining the aspects of Social Entrepreneurship
  2. Participants will be given a product along with a sample document.
  3. Participants will be expected to mail their solution to the organizers exactly after 40 mins
  4. Top Selected Teams will be announced on the same day.
  5. Selected Teams will be given a problem statement on Social Entrepreneurship and asked for a solution.
  6. Solution to the problem statement will be pitched in front of the judges

Financial Planning in Student Life

 25th April, 2021    4:00 pm

A webinar that shows how financial planning helps the student see the world for what it is, and that is, to always live according to their means. The session will emphasize on how planning early or how just being financially educated early in life will take you places. There is this adage that goes, those who fail to plan, plan to fail. Financial planning as a student will not only help you tackle present economic challenges, but those, that will be coming in subsequent times.

Financial Planning in Student Life

Financial Planning in Student Life "College is a great time to learn to live within your means",says Eric Roberge As a college student, financial planning is probably the last thing on our mind. College is seen as a time for finding our passion, charting our career path and, of course, partying. But financial decisions we make in college will reverberate through our life for years, even decades Financial Planning at an Early stage is extremely crucial as it decides a lot of things— from what kind of car we can afford, how much we can save for retirement to whether we can buy a home. Minimizing our debt now will make life much easier once we are out of college. BloomBox wants to throw light on this undervalued asset which once understood can work wonders.

Topics to be Covered in the Webinar:

  • Topics to be Covered in the Webinar
  • Earning Money and FIrst Job
  • Discussion on Financial Rights
  • Managing our Money
  • Credit Card - Should we get one?
  • Savings and Investment plans and methods
  • Financial Discipline, Financial Independence, Financial Prudence and Pros of Financial Planning

Frequently Asked Questions



What is E-Summit?

BloomBox is here with the first ever E-summit of KJSCE to promote Entrepreneurship among young minds.

Who can be a part of this E-Summit?

All undergrads can participate in the Summit.

Is there any registration fees for any event of E-Summit?

No, there's no registration fees for any of the event as we are a Non-Profit Organization.

Is there a limit on number of events once can participate in?

No, there's no limit on the number of events one can participate in.

Will the events be held online or offline?

The entire E-Summit will be held in an online mode.

Should all team members be of same college for any event?

No, there's no such necessity but all the team members should be undergrads.

On what platforms will the events be held on?

Events will be held on various platforms like Google Meet, Zoom and Microsoft Teams.
